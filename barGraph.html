Trends in PM2.5 concentrations (1990-2020)
This interactive graph provides a detailed comparison of annual PM2.5 (particulate matter less than 2.5 micrometers) concentrations measured in countries over a thirty-year period from 1990 to 2020. PM2.5 particles are known for their ability to penetrate deep into the respiratory tract, posing significant health risks, including increased mortality from cardiovascular and respiratory diseases, as well as lung cancer.
